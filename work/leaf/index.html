Leaf is a support network for Indigenous mothers with a smoking habit. The client neeeded a culturaly-appropriate online space for Indigenous mothers to feel safe and motivated to stop smoking. The content-strategy focused on a holistic approach to better-health, and encouraged other health aspects.

The App provided motivating content, photo-uploads and instant feedback to new and experienced mothers.
