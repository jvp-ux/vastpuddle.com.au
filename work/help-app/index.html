HeLP App

A better way to help your back

The HeLP App and helpmyback.org.au website is a book-replacement project for patients with chronic book pain. Our client wanted to measure use and education metrics for patients as they engage with the App.

The HeLP App tool guides patients and records the usefulness of the information they access. The metrics are then used to change-up the communication, content and consultation frequency.
